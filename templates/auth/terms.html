Comolor Pharmacy Management System provides comprehensive pharmacy management software for Kenyan chemists and pharmacies. Our services include inventory management, point-of-sale systems, customer management, reporting, and licensing management.

License expires 30 days after activation. Expired licenses will result in system access being blocked until renewal.
